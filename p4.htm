About Me

Hi! My name is Erin Rodriguez and I've been drawing for as long as I can remember. Some of my favorite generes to make characters for include fantasy, western, and superheroes. I got into character design becasue as a kid I yearned for the charaters in Saturday morning cartoons to look like me. So I took my mother's words to heart: "If you don't see what you like do it yourself!"
